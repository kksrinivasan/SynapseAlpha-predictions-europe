# Retraction: week-europe-2026-W15

**Date:** April 15, 2026
**Edition retracted:** week-europe-2026-W15 (published April 9, 2026)

## Reason

During a routine data quality audit, we identified that the European fundamental dataset used during model calibration contained inaccuracies. As a result, we have decided to retract this edition.

## Status

- EU predictions are paused while we rebuild the European dataset with verified fundamentals
- EU predictions will resume once data quality has been confirmed end-to-end
- This edition should not be included in track record evaluation

This retraction reflects our commitment to transparency and data integrity. We would rather publish nothing than publish on data we cannot fully stand behind.
